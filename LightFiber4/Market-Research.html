Research Insights

Streetlights have high energy consumption rate.

Expose wires pose threats nearby people.

It is difficult to maintain and manage.

A defected apparatus could cause damage to nearby property.

Proposed Solution

•The Smart light features a tilt sensor to sense any structure damage caused by possible car collision or if the light itself has been tilted from failure to mount properly
•It also allows for quick repairs from the fast notification system that will send a repair status update to a webpage which allows the repairmen to be alerted of whichever smart light has been damaged
•The repair notification system will be implemented and connected to a webpage
