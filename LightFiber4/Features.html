LightFiber

Our Objective

We developed a reliable street light that is easier to manage and prioritises the safety of people nearby. Our street lights will be reliable and have reduced chances of having a faulty one. It'll also help save energy comsumption by reducing the brightness of the light depending on certain weather conditions.

Features

Features in our Smart StreetLight

Tilt Sensor

A tilt sensor that would detect when there's an accident. We will be notified when our streetlight is hit or damaged.

Temperature Sensor

A temperature sensor that will help change the brightness of our light depending on the weather conditions, helping us save energy.

LED Light

A smart LED light that would turn off and on depending on the time.

Database Webpage

A webpage that will update us the condition status of each of our street lights.
